Company Overview

Faisal Al Bahlouli Trading & Contracting LLC is a leading multi-disciplinary trading and contracting company based in the Sultanate of Oman, specializing in the supply of high-quality electrical, construction, industrial, and infrastructure solutions. Since its establishment, the company has been committed to delivering reliable products and professional services that meet the evolving demands of the construction, industrial, commercial, and government sectors.

With over 12 years of industry experience, we have built a strong reputation for quality, reliability, technical expertise, and customer satisfaction. Our extensive product portfolio includes electrical and power solutions, construction materials, piping systems, industrial equipment, safety products, and engineering support services sourced from trusted global manufacturers and industry-leading brands.

At Faisal Al Bahlouli Trading & Contracting LLC, we understand the importance of timely delivery, cost-effective solutions, and uncompromising quality standards. Our experienced team works closely with clients to provide tailored solutions that enhance operational efficiency, project performance, and long-term value.

Serving as a vital link between international suppliers and local markets, we support major infrastructure developments, industrial facilities, commercial projects, and maintenance operations across Oman. Through our commitment to excellence, innovation, and integrity, we continue to contribute to the nation's growing industrial and construction landscape while building lasting partnerships with our customers and stakeholders.
